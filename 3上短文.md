# 三年级上册英语短文

## 1. 学校的一天

**Title: A Day at School**

**Body:**
Hello, kids! Today is a sunny day. We begin our English class at eight. Ms Wang says, "Let's start!" We listen, speak, and sing songs. I can draw a cat on the paper. My friend Tom is clever. He can act out the story. After class, we play in the playground. We run, jump, and skip rope. We all have fun. School is great!

**中文大意:**
孩子们好！今天是晴天。我们八点开始英语课。王老师说："让我们开始吧！"我们听、说、唱歌。我可以在纸上画一只猫。我的朋友汤姆很聪明。他可以表演这个故事。下课后，我们在操场上玩。我们跑、跳、跳绳。我们都很开心。学校太棒了！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
hello, kids, today, begin, English, class, eight, Ms, let, start, listen, speak, sing, draw, cat, paper, friend, clever, act, story, play, playground, run, jump, skip, rope, all, fun, school, great

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时
- 祈使句（Let's...）
- 情态动词can的用法

## 2. 我的家庭和周末

**Title: My Family and Our Weekend**

**Body:**
I love my family. There are six people: father, mother, brother, sister, grandma, and me. My father is tall. My mother cooks nice food. My brother likes to play with a ball. My sister draws colorful pictures. My grandma tells me stories. On weekends, we often go to the park. We take a ball. My father plays football. My mother sits on a bench. My sister draws pictures. I see a duck in the pond. It's swimming. I also see a bird in the tree. It's singing. We have a picnic. We eat some apples. We are a happy family!

**中文大意:**
我爱我的家人。有六口人：爸爸、妈妈、哥哥、妹妹、奶奶和我。我爸爸很高。我妈妈做美味的食物。我哥哥喜欢玩球。我妹妹画彩色的画。我奶奶给我讲故事。周末，我们经常去公园。我们带了一个球。我爸爸踢足球。我妈妈坐在长椅上。我妹妹画画。我看到池塘里有一只鸭子。它在游泳。我还看到树上有一只鸟。它在唱歌。我们野餐。我们吃了一些苹果。我们是一个幸福的家庭！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
love, family, father, mother, brother, sister, grandma, me, tall, cooks, nice, food, play, ball, draws, colorful, pictures, tells, stories, weekend, go, park, take, sit, bench, see, duck, pond, swimming, bird, tree, singing, have, picnic, eat, some, apple, happy

**语法点:**
- there be句型
- 一般现在时
- 描述家庭和周末活动

## 3. 颜色和我们的教室

**Title: Colors and Our Classroom**

**Body:**
Look at the rainbow! It has red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. The sky is blue. The grass is green. The sun is yellow. My schoolbag is purple. My pencil case is pink. Welcome to our classroom! It's big and clean. There are many desks and chairs. The blackboard is on the wall. The window is beside the door. My schoolbag is on my desk. I have a pencil, a pen, and an eraser. The teacher is writing on the blackboard. We are listening carefully. We love our classroom!

**中文大意:**
看彩虹！它有红、橙、黄、绿、蓝、靛、紫。天空是蓝色的。草是绿色的。太阳是黄色的。我的书包是紫色的。我的铅笔盒是粉色的。欢迎来到我们的教室！它又大又干净。有很多桌子和椅子。黑板在墙上。窗户在门旁边。我的书包在我的桌子上。我有一支铅笔、一支钢笔和一块橡皮。老师在黑板上写字。我们在认真听。我们爱我们的教室！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
look, rainbow, red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, violet, sky, grass, sun, schoolbag, purple, pencil case, pink, welcome, classroom, big, clean, many, desk, chair, blackboard, wall, window, beside, door, pencil, pen, eraser, teacher, writing, listening, love

**语法点:**
- 祈使句（Look at...）
- there be句型
- 方位介词的使用
- 现在进行时

## 4. 运动日和我的朋友

**Title: Sports Day and My Friends**

**Body:**
Today is Sports Day. We are all ready. I run fast. My friend Amy jumps high. Tom plays football. Lily skips with a rope. We all try our best. The teacher says, "Well done!" We are happy. I have three friends: Tom, Amy, and Lily. Tom is tall. Amy is clever. Lily is lovely. We play together every day. We play football. We skip rope. We draw pictures. We have a lot of fun. They are my best friends.

**中文大意:**
今天是运动日。我们都准备好了。我跑得快。我的朋友艾米跳得高。汤姆踢足球。莉莉用绳子跳绳。我们都尽了最大努力。老师说："做得好！"我们很高兴。我有三个朋友：汤姆、艾米和莉莉。汤姆很高。艾米很聪明。莉莉很可爱。我们每天一起玩。我们踢足球。我们跳绳。我们画画。我们玩得很开心。他们是我最好的朋友。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
today, Sports Day, all, ready, run, fast, friend, Amy, jump, high, Tom, play, football, Lily, skip, rope, try, best, teacher, well done, happy, three, tall, clever, lovely, together, every, day, draw, picture, lot, fun, best

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时
- 副词的使用（fast, high）
- 固定短语（try one's best, well done）

## 5. 在家的一天和我的生日

**Title: A Day at Home and My Birthday**

**Body:**
Hi, I'm Tom. Today is Saturday. I get up at seven. My dad makes breakfast. My mum drinks tea. I have a little dog. It's very lovely. We play in the garden. I see a butterfly. It's colorful. My brother comes home. He has a skipping rope. We skip together. It's fun! Tomorrow is my birthday. My friends will come to my party. I will get many gifts. My best friend will give me a toy car. My sister will give me a paintbrush. We will sing "Happy Birthday" to me. We will eat a big cake. We will play games. We will have a lot of fun.

**中文大意:**
嗨，我是汤姆。今天是星期六。我七点起床。我爸爸做早餐。我妈妈喝茶。我有一只小狗。它很可爱。我们在花园里玩。我看到一只蝴蝶。它是彩色的。我哥哥回家了。他有一根跳绳。我们一起跳绳。很有趣！明天是我的生日。我的朋友会来参加我的派对。我会收到很多礼物。我最好的朋友会给我一辆玩具车。我妹妹会给我一支画笔。我们会给我唱"生日快乐"歌。我们会吃一个大蛋糕。我们会玩游戏。我们会玩得很开心。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
hi, I'm, Tom, today, Saturday, get, up, seven, dad, make, breakfast, mum, drink, tea, little, dog, lovely, play, garden, see, butterfly, colorful, brother, come, home, skipping rope, skip, together, fun, tomorrow, birthday, friend, party, get, many, gift, best, give, toy, car, sister, paintbrush, sing, Happy Birthday, eat, cake, game, lot

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时
- 一般将来时
- 时间表达
- 描述日常活动和生日派对

## 6. 动物园和雨天

**Title: At the Zoo and A Rainy Day**

**Body:**
Today, I go to the zoo with my family. I see a tiger. It's big and strong. I also see a monkey. It's clever. It can jump high. I see a bird. It's colorful. I see a fish. It's swimming in the pond. I have a lot of fun at the zoo. I love animals! The next day, it's a rainy day. I can't go out to play. I stay at home. I read a book. I draw pictures. I play with my dog. My mum makes hot tea for me. I love rainy days. They are quiet and cozy.

**中文大意:**
今天，我和我的家人去动物园。我看到一只老虎。它又大又强壮。我还看到一只猴子。它很聪明。它能跳得很高。我看到一只鸟。它是彩色的。我看到一条鱼。它在池塘里游泳。我在动物园玩得很开心。我爱动物！第二天，是雨天。我不能出去玩。我待在家里。我读一本书。我画画。我和我的狗玩。我妈妈给我做了热茶。我爱雨天。它们安静而舒适。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
today, go, zoo, family, see, tiger, big, strong, monkey, clever, jump, high, bird, colorful, fish, swimming, pond, lot, fun, love, animal, next, day, rainy, can't, go, out, play, stay, home, read, book, draw, picture, play, dog, mum, make, hot, tea, quiet, cozy

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时
- 描述动物、天气和活动

## 7. 我的书包和我们的旅行

**Title: My School Bag and Our Trip**

**Body:**
I have a new schoolbag. It's blue and big. I put my books, pencil case, and ruler in it. My pencil case is pink. It has pens, pencils, and an eraser. I love my schoolbag. It's very useful. I take it to school every day. Last weekend, we went on a trip to the mountains. We took our schoolbags with us. We brought food, water, and toys. We climbed the mountain. We saw beautiful flowers and trees. We had a picnic at the top. We played games and sang songs. It was a great trip!

**中文大意:**
我有一个新书包。它是蓝色的，很大。我把我的书、铅笔盒和尺子放在里面。我的铅笔盒是粉色的。它有钢笔、铅笔和橡皮。我爱我的书包。它非常有用。我每天都带着它去学校。上周末，我们去山区旅行。我们带着书包。我们带了食物、水和玩具。我们爬了山。我们看到了美丽的花和树。我们在山顶野餐。我们玩游戏，唱歌。这是一次很棒的旅行！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
have, new, schoolbag, blue, big, put, book, pencil case, ruler, pink, pen, pencil, eraser, love, useful, take, school, every, day, last, weekend, trip, mountain, take, bring, food, water, toy, climb, see, beautiful, flower, tree, have, picnic, top, play, game, sing, song, great

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时
- 一般过去时
- 描述物品和旅行活动
