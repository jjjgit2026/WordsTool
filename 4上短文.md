# 四年级上册英语短文

## 1. 星空之夜冒险

**Title: The Starry Night Adventure**

**Body:**
Tom loves to look at the stars every night. One evening, he sees a very bright star in the sky. "Wow, that star is really beautiful!" he says. Suddenly, a little wind blows, and Tom hears a sound. It's a band playing music nearby. Tom runs fast to see the band. They are playing happy songs. Tom dances to the music. Then, he sees his friend Lily. They play ping-pong together. Tom is very good at ping-pong. He wins the game. "Are you sure you don't want to play again?" Lily asks. "No, I'm tired," Tom says. They sit down and look at the stars again. "I think stars are magical," Tom says. "Me too," Lily agrees. They have a wonderful night.

**中文大意:**
汤姆喜欢每晚看星星。一天晚上，他看到天空中有一颗非常明亮的星星。"哇，那颗星星真漂亮！"他说。突然，一阵微风吹来，汤姆听到了声音。是附近有乐队在演奏音乐。汤姆快速跑去看乐队。他们在演奏快乐的歌曲。汤姆随着音乐跳舞。然后，他看到了他的朋友莉莉。他们一起打乒乓球。汤姆乒乓球打得很好。他赢了比赛。"你确定不想再玩一次吗？"莉莉问。"不，我累了，"汤姆说。他们坐下来再次看星星。"我认为星星是神奇的，"汤姆说。"我也是，"莉莉同意。他们度过了一个美好的夜晚。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
star, ping-pong, band, very, fast, sure, think, really, wind

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时和一般过去时的使用
- 形容词的用法（bright, beautiful, happy, good, tired, wonderful）
- 感叹句的使用（Wow, that star is really beautiful!）

## 2. 小镇之旅和公交车程

**Title: The Town Tour and Bus Ride**

**Body:**
Today is Sunday. Amy and her family go on a tour of their town. First, they go to the train station. They see a big train there. Then, they walk to the supermarket. Amy's mom buys some fruits and vegetables. Next, they go to the cinema. They watch a funny movie. After that, they take the bus to the hospital. Amy's grandma works there. They say hello to her. Then, they go to the police station. A policeman tells them about his job. Finally, they go to a shop. Amy buys a new toy. They have a great day!

The next day, Tom takes the bus to school. He waits at the bus stop. The bus arrives. He gets on the bus. He sits next to his friend Mike. They talk about their weekend. They see a hospital, a police station, and a supermarket. The bus stops at the school. They get off the bus. They go to their classroom. Their teacher is waiting for them. They say hello to their teacher. They sit down and take out their books. They start their lessons. They have a busy day at school. They take the bus home after school. They are tired but happy.

**中文大意:**
今天是星期天。艾米和她的家人去他们的小镇旅游。首先，他们去火车站。他们在那里看到一列大火车。然后，他们步行去超市。艾米的妈妈买了一些水果和蔬菜。接下来，他们去电影院。他们看了一部有趣的电影。之后，他们乘公共汽车去医院。艾米的奶奶在那里工作。他们向她问好。然后，他们去警察局。一位警察告诉他们关于他的工作。最后，他们去了一家商店。艾米买了一个新玩具。他们度过了愉快的一天！

第二天，汤姆乘公共汽车上学。他在公共汽车站等车。公共汽车来了。他上了公共汽车。他坐在他的朋友迈克旁边。他们谈论他们的周末。他们看到一家医院、一个警察局和一家超市。公共汽车在学校停下来。他们下了公共汽车。他们去他们的教室。他们的老师在等他们。他们向他们的老师问好。他们坐下，拿出他们的书。他们开始上课。他们在学校度过了忙碌的一天。他们放学后乘公共汽车回家。他们很累但很开心。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**

**语法点:**

## 4. 形状寻宝和地图冒险

**Title: The Shape Hunt and Map Adventure**

**Body:**
Today, the students are on a shape hunt. They look for different shapes around the school. First, they see a triangle roof on the library. Then, they find a circle clock on the wall. Next, they see a rectangle door and square windows in the classroom. They also find a triangle pyramid in the science room. The teacher asks them to draw the shapes they find. The students have a lot of fun. They learn that shapes are everywhere!

Tom and his friends are going on a treasure hunt. They use a map. The map has a big X on it. They follow the map. First, they go straight for 100 meters. Then, they turn left. Next, they go straight for 50 meters. Then, they turn right. They see a tree. They dig under the tree. They find a box. They open the box. It has a toy car, a chocolate bar, and a note. The note says, "You are the best treasure hunters!" They are very happy. They share the chocolate. They have a lot of fun.

**中文大意:**
今天，学生们在进行形状寻宝。他们在学校周围寻找不同的形状。首先，他们看到图书馆的三角形屋顶。然后，他们在墙上找到一个圆形的时钟。接下来，他们看到教室里有一扇矩形的门和方形的窗户。他们还在科学室里发现了一个三角形的金字塔。老师让他们画出他们找到的形状。学生们玩得很开心。他们了解到形状无处不在！

汤姆和他的朋友们正在进行寻宝游戏。他们使用地图。地图上有一个大X。他们按照地图走。首先，他们直走100米。然后，他们左转。接下来，他们直走50米。然后，他们右转。他们看到一棵树。他们在树下挖。他们发现一个盒子。他们打开盒子。里面有一辆玩具车、一块巧克力和一张纸条。纸条上写着："你们是最好的寻宝者！"他们非常开心。他们分享巧克力。他们玩得很开心。

He shears the sheep. He plows the fields. He plants seeds. He harvests the crops. He is very busy. But he is happy. He loves his farm. He loves his animals. He says, "Farming is a good life."
