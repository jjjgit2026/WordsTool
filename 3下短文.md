# 三年级下册英语短文

## 1. 我的感受

**Title: My Feelings**

**Body:**
Today is Monday. I wake up feeling tired. I don't want to go to school. But I have to. At school, we have a math test. I feel worried. I study hard but I'm scared I might fail. After the test, my friend Tom invites me to play football. I feel excited. We play and laugh. I forget about the test. When I get home, my mom gives me a big hug. I feel happy. My dad tells me the test results will be okay. I feel better. It's a day of many feelings!

**中文大意:**
今天是星期一。我醒来感觉很累。我不想上学。但我必须去。在学校，我们有数学考试。我感到担心。我努力学习，但害怕可能会不及格。考试后，我的朋友汤姆邀请我踢足球。我感到兴奋。我们玩得很开心，笑个不停。我忘记了考试。当我回家时，妈妈给了我一个大大的拥抱。我感到开心。爸爸告诉我考试结果会好的。我感觉好多了。这是充满各种感受的一天！

## 2. 我的身体和我

**Title: My Body and Me**

**Body:**
Hi, I'm Lily. I have a head, two eyes, two ears, a nose, and a mouth. I have long hair. I have two arms and two hands. I have two legs and two feet. I use my eyes to see. I use my ears to hear. I use my nose to smell. I use my mouth to eat and talk. I use my hands to write and draw. I use my feet to walk and run. I take care of my body. I wash my face every morning. I brush my teeth every night. I exercise every day. I stretch my arms and legs. I shake hands with my friends. I wave to my family. I love my body!

**中文大意:**
嗨，我是莉莉。我有一个头，两只眼睛，两只耳朵，一个鼻子和一张嘴。我有长头发。我有两只胳膊和两只手。我有两条腿和两只脚。我用眼睛看。我用耳朵听。我用鼻子闻。我用嘴吃饭和说话。我用手写字和画画。我用脚走路和跑步。我照顾我的身体。我每天早上洗脸。我每晚刷牙。我每天锻炼。我伸展胳膊和腿。我和朋友握手。我向家人挥手。我爱我的身体！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
head, eye, hair, ear, nose, face, arm, hand, mouth, tooth, body, leg, foot, touch, wave, shake, wash, stretch, care, exercise, see, hear, smell, eat, talk, write, draw, walk, run, love

**语法点:**
- 描述身体部位及其功能
- 一般现在时
- 使用use to do结构

## 3. 我的衣服

**Title: My Clothes**

**Body:**
I have many clothes. I have a blue dress, a red coat, and a green sweater. I have black trousers, white shorts, and blue jeans. I have pink socks, brown boots, and white shoes. I have a yellow cap, a purple hat, and a shiny robe. I wear different clothes every day. On Monday, I wear a T shirt and jeans. On Tuesday, I wear a dress and shoes. On Wednesday, I wear a sweater and trousers. On Thursday, I wear a coat and boots. On Friday, I wear shorts and sandals. On Saturday, I wear a robe at home. On Sunday, I wear nice clothes to church. I love my clothes!

## 4. 四季

**Title: The Four Seasons**

**Body:**
There are four seasons in a year: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. In spring, the weather is warm. Flowers bloom. Birds sing. I fly kites in the park. In summer, the weather is hot. The sun shines. I swim in the pool. I eat ice cream. In autumn, the weather is cool. Leaves fall. I pick apples. I draw pictures. In winter, the weather is cold. It snows. I make snowballs. I throw them with my friends. I wear a warm coat and boots. I love all the seasons!

**中文大意:**
一年有四个季节：春天、夏天、秋天和冬天。春天，天气温暖。花儿绽放。鸟儿歌唱。我在公园放风筝。夏天，天气炎热。太阳照耀。我在游泳池游泳。我吃冰淇淋。秋天，天气凉爽。树叶落下。我摘苹果。我画画。冬天，天气寒冷。下雪了。我做雪球。我和朋友一起扔雪球。我穿暖和的外套和靴子。我爱所有的季节！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
spring, summer, autumn, winter, season, weather, warm, hot, cool, cold, sun, shine, rain, rainy, snow, snowy, wind, windy, fly, kite, park, swim, pool, ice cream, leaf, fall, pick, apple, draw, picture, make, snowball, throw, wear, coat, boot, love, year, flower, bloom, bird, sing, friend

**语法点:**
- 描述季节和天气

## 5. 我的一周

**Title: My Week**

**Body:**
My week is busy but fun. On Monday, I go to school. I have math and English. On Tuesday, I have science and art. On Wednesday, I play basketball after school. On Thursday, I go to the bookshop. On Friday, I have a music class. On Saturday, I stay at home. I clean my room. I wash my clothes. I hang them to dry. On Sunday, I go to the park with my family. We fly kites. We have a picnic. I love my week!

**中文大意:**
我的一周忙碌但有趣。星期一，我上学。我有数学和英语课。星期二，我有科学和美术课。星期三，我放学后打篮球。星期四，我去书店。星期五，我有音乐课。星期六，我待在家里。我打扫房间。我洗衣服。我把它们挂起来晾干。星期日，我和家人去公园。我们放风筝。我们野餐。我爱我的一周！

## 6. 我最喜欢的食物

**Title: My Favorite Food**

**Body:**
I love food! My favorite food is Chinese food. I like rice, noodles, and dumplings. I love dumplings with meat and vegetables. My mom makes yummy dumplings. She uses dumpling wrappers, meat, carrots, and mushrooms. She adds salt and oil. I help her mix the filling. I also like pizza, chicken, and ice cream. I eat an apple every day. I drink milk and juice. I eat vegetables like tomatoes and potatoes. I eat fruit like bananas and grapes. I love food!

**中文大意:**
我喜欢食物！我最喜欢的食物是中国菜。我喜欢米饭、面条和饺子。我喜欢有肉和蔬菜的饺子。我妈妈做的饺子很好吃。她用饺子皮、肉、胡萝卜和蘑菇。她加盐和油。我帮她混合馅料。我也喜欢披萨、鸡肉和冰淇淋。我每天吃一个苹果。我喝牛奶和果汁。我吃西红柿和土豆等蔬菜。我吃香蕉和葡萄等水果。我喜欢食物！

## 7. 在家的一天

**Title: A Day at Home**

**Body:**
Today is Saturday. I stay at home. I wake up late. I have breakfast: bread, egg, and milk. I clean my room. I wash my clothes. I hang them to dry. I help my mom cook lunch. We make soup and rice. I add salt to the soup. After lunch, I do my homework. I study math and English. I draw a picture. I play with my cat. I read a book. I listen to music. I have dinner: noodles and vegetables. I watch TV. I go to bed early. It's a nice day at home!

**中文大意:**
今天是星期六。我待在家里。我起得很晚。我吃早餐：面包、鸡蛋和牛奶。我打扫房间。我洗衣服。我把它们挂起来晾干。我帮妈妈做午饭。我们做汤和米饭。我往汤里加盐。午饭后，我做作业。我学习数学和英语。我画一幅画。我和我的猫玩。我读一本书。我听音乐。我吃晚餐：面条和蔬菜。我看电视。我早睡。这是在家的美好一天！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**

**语法点:**
