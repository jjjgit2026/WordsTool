# 四年级下册英语短文

## 1. 一天的时间安排

**Title: A Day in My Life**

**Body:**
I wake up at 7:00 am every morning. I brush my teeth and wash my face. Then I have breakfast with my family. At half past eight, I go to school. We have four lessons in the morning. At lunchtime, we eat lunch at school. In the afternoon, we have art and PE lessons. School ends at 3:00 pm. I go home and do my homework. In the evening, I watch TV with my family. I go to bed at 9:00 pm. It's a busy but happy day!

**中文大意:**
我每天早上7点起床。我刷牙洗脸。然后和家人一起吃早餐。八点半，我去上学。上午我们有四节课。午餐时间，我们在学校吃午饭。下午，我们有美术和体育课。学校下午3点放学。我回家做作业。晚上，我和家人一起看电视。我晚上9点上床睡觉。这是忙碌但快乐的一天！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
am, pm, evening, breakfast, lunchtime, half past eight, lesson, art, time

**语法点:**
- 时间表达法
- 日常活动的描述

## 2. 四季的变化

**Title: Four Seasons**

**Body:**
There are four seasons in a year. Spring comes in March. The trees turn green and flowers bloom. Summer is from June to August. It's hot, and we can go to the beach. Autumn starts in September. The leaves turn yellow and fall. Winter is from December to February. It snows in the north, and we can make snowmen. Each season is beautiful in its own way.

**中文大意:**
一年有四个季节。春天在三月到来。树木变绿，花儿盛开。夏天从六月到八月。天气炎热，我们可以去海滩。秋天从九月开始。树叶变黄并掉落。冬天从十二月到二月。北方下雪，我们可以堆雪人。每个季节都有自己的美丽。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
year, March, June, July, August, September, December, February, beach, snow, snowman

**语法点:**
- 季节的表达
- 月份的名称

## 3. 家庭搬家

**Title: Moving to a New Home**

**Body:**
Last month, my family moved to a new house. The new house is bigger than the old one. It has a living room, a kitchen, two bedrooms, and a bathroom. In the living room, there is a sofa, a table, and a TV. In my bedroom, there is a bed, a bookshelf, and a lamp. I like my new room very much. My friends came to visit me. We played together happily. It's a nice new home!

**中文大意:**
上个月，我的家人搬到了新房子。新房子比旧房子大。它有一个客厅、一个厨房、两个卧室和一个浴室。在客厅里，有一张沙发、一张桌子和一台电视。在我的卧室里，有一张床、一个书架和一盏台灯。我非常喜欢我的新房间。我的朋友们来看我。我们一起开心地玩。这是一个很好的新家！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
move, living room, kitchen, bedroom, bathroom, sofa, table, bed, bookshelf, lamp

**语法点:**
- 描述房间和家具
- 过去时态的使用

## 4. 学校旅行

**Title: School Trip**

**Body:**
Last week, we had a school trip to the museum. We left school at 9:00 am. The museum is two kilometres away from school. We saw many interesting things there, like old photos and dinosaur bones. We learned about the history of our city. We also visited a nature reserve near the museum. We saw many plants and birds. We had lunch at the museum café. We went back to school at 3:00 pm. It was a fantastic trip!

**中文大意:**
上周，我们学校组织了一次去博物馆的旅行。我们上午9点离开学校。博物馆离学校有两公里远。我们在那里看到了许多有趣的东西，比如旧照片和恐龙骨头。我们了解了我们城市的历史。我们还参观了博物馆附近的自然保护区。我们看到了许多植物和鸟类。我们在博物馆的咖啡馆吃了午饭。我们下午3点回到学校。这是一次很棒的旅行！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
trip, museum, kilometre, photo, dinosaur, bone, history, plant, nature reserve, together

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 学校活动的描述

## 5. 自然保护区之旅

**Title: A Trip to the Nature Reserve**

**Body:**
Last weekend, my family went to a nature reserve. It's a big forest with many trees and animals. We saw swallows flying in the sky. We heard larks singing beautifully. We walked along the path and saw a beech tree. It's very tall, about 10 metres high. We also saw a cave in the hill. We were careful not to get lost. We had a picnic lunch there. We took many photos. It was a wonderful day in nature!

**中文大意:**
上周末，我的家人去了自然保护区。那是一个有许多树木和动物的大森林。我们看到燕子在天空中飞翔。我们听到云雀优美地歌唱。我们沿着小路走，看到了一棵山毛榉树。它很高，大约10米高。我们还在山上看到了一个山洞。我们小心翼翼地不让自己迷路。我们在那里野餐。我们拍了很多照片。在大自然中度过了美好的一天！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
nature reserve, forest, swallow, lark, beech, metre, cave, hill, careful, photo

**语法点:**
- 自然环境的描述
- 过去时态的使用

We also have a drawing contest in the art room. At lunchtime, we eat salad and fruits. In the afternoon, we go to the library to read books. We have a good time together. School activity day is always super fun!

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**

## 7. 冬季的乐趣

**Title: Winter Fun**

**Body:**
Winter is my favourite season. It snows a lot in my city. I can make snowmen with my friends. We use carrots for the nose and buttons for the eyes. We also have snowball fights. It's very exciting. Sometimes we go skating on the lake. On Christmas Eve, we decorate the Christmas tree. We sing songs and exchange gifts. We have a big dinner with family. Winter is full of fun!

**中文大意:**
冬天是我最喜欢的季节。我的城市经常下雪。我可以和朋友们一起堆雪人。我们用胡萝卜做鼻子，用纽扣做眼睛。我们还打雪仗。这非常令人兴奋。有时我们去湖上滑冰。平安夜，我们装饰圣诞树。我们唱歌并交换礼物。我们和家人一起吃丰盛的晚餐。冬天充满了乐趣！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
snow, snowman, fan, loudly, gift, together, happy

**语法点:**
- 冬季活动的描述
- 现在时态的使用

## 8. 恐龙博物馆之旅

**Title: Dinosaur Museum Trip**

**Body:**
Last Sunday, my class visited the dinosaur museum. We saw many dinosaur skeletons and bones. The guide told us about different kinds of dinosaurs. Suddenly, there was a storm outside. We stayed in the museum and watched a dinosaur movie. It was very interesting. We learned that dinosaurs lived millions of years ago. We bought tickets for the museum shop and got some dinosaur toys. We had a great time. We will remember this trip forever!

**中文大意:**
上周日，我们班参观了恐龙博物馆。我们看到了许多恐龙骨架和骨头。导游告诉我们关于不同种类的恐龙。突然，外面下起了暴风雨。我们留在博物馆里看了一部恐龙电影。它非常有趣。我们了解到恐龙生活在数百万年前。我们买了博物馆商店的门票，得到了一些恐龙玩具。我们玩得很开心。我们会永远记住这次旅行！

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
dinosaur, bone, suddenly, storm, cave, ticket, guide, history, remember

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 博物馆参观的描述

## 11. 时间管理

**Title: Time Management**

**Body:**
I have a clock in my room. It helps me manage my time. I wake up at 7:00 am every day. I have breakfast at 7:30. I go to school at 8:00. School starts at half past eight. I have four lessons in the morning. At fifteen past twelve, I have lunch. I have two lessons in the afternoon. School ends at 3:00 pm. I do my homework from 4:00 to 5:30. I play with friends from 5:30 to 6:30. I have dinner at 7:00. I watch TV from 7:30 to 8:30. I go to bed at 9:00. I beat the clock every day!

**中文大意:**
我的房间里有一个时钟。它帮助我管理时间。我每天早上7点起床。我7:30吃早餐。我8点去上学。学校八点半开始上课。上午我有四节课。12点15分，我吃午饭。下午我有两节课。学校下午3点放学。我从4点到5:30做作业。我从5:30到6:30和朋友玩。我7点吃晚饭。我从7:30到8:30看电视。我9点上床睡觉。我每天都能提前完成任务！

**语法点:**
- 时间表达法

**Title: School Rules**

**Body:**

We mustn't run in the hallways. We mustn't talk loudly in the library. We mustn't eat in the classroom. We must be friendly to each other. Following rules helps us have a good school life.

**中文大意:**
我们学校有很多规则。我们必须准时到达学校。我们必须穿校服。我们必须听从老师的指示。我们必须保持教室干净整洁。我们不能在走廊里跑。我们不能在图书馆里大声说话。我们不能在教室里吃东西。我们必须对彼此友好。遵守规则帮助我们有良好的学校生活。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
rule, follow, time, tidy, friendly, must

**语法点:**
- 规则的表达
- 情态动词的使用
