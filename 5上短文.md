# 五年级上册英语短文

## 1. 暑假冒险记

**Title: The Summer Holiday Adventure**

**Body:**
It was summer holiday, and Lily was very excited. She went to New South Wales with her family. The scenery there was beautiful! They took photos of the opera house and the beach. Lily collected many shiny shells. One day, they met a farmer who taught them how to pick plums. They had a fantastic time. Soon, it was time to go back home. Lily promised to write emails to her new friends there.

**中文大意:**
暑假到了，莉莉非常兴奋。她和家人去了新南威尔士州。那里的风景非常美丽！他们拍摄了歌剧院和海滩的照片。莉莉收集了许多闪亮的贝壳。有一天，他们遇到了一位农民，他教他们如何摘李子。他们玩得很开心。很快，就到了回家的时间。莉莉承诺会给那里的新朋友发电子邮件。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
summer holiday, New South Wales, beautiful, take photos, opera house, collect, shiny, shell, farmer, pick, plum, fantastic, soon, email

**语法点:**
- 一般过去时的使用
- 简单过去式动词形式

## 2. 家庭聚会

**Title: Family Gathering**

**Body:**
Last weekend, Tom's family had a big gathering. His grandparents, uncles, aunts, and cousins all came. His grandmother, or granny, cooked delicious food. His grandfather told funny stories about his youth. Tom introduced his new friend Jack to everyone. They played games together. Tom's cousin showed him her soft toys collection. It was a wonderful time. Everyone felt happy and loved.

**中文大意:**
上周末，汤姆的家人举行了一次大型聚会。他的祖父母、叔叔、阿姨和表兄弟姐妹们都来了。他的祖母（奶奶）做了美味的食物。他的祖父讲了关于他年轻时的有趣故事。汤姆向大家介绍了他的新朋友杰克。他们一起玩游戏。汤姆的表妹向他展示了她的毛绒玩具收藏。那是一段美好的时光。每个人都感到快乐和被爱。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
weekend, family, grandfather, grandmother, granny, cook, funny, cousin, introduce, soft toy, collect

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 简单句的组合

## 3. 校园戏剧表演

**Title: School Play**

**Body:**
The school was putting on a play. Amy got the role of Little Red Riding Hood. She practiced hard every day. The scene was set in the woods. On the day of the performance, the school bell rang, and everyone took their seats. Amy walked through the woods, and then met a wolf. She didn't run away but was brave. The play was a big success. Everyone clapped loudly. Amy felt proud of herself.

**中文大意:**
学校正在上演一场戏剧。艾米获得了小红帽的角色。她每天努力练习。场景设置在森林里。表演那天，学校铃声响起，大家都坐好了。艾米穿过森林，然后遇到了一只狼。她没有逃跑，而是很勇敢。戏剧非常成功。大家都大声鼓掌。艾米为自己感到骄傲。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
school, role, Little Red Riding Hood, hard, scene, woods, bell, run away, success

**语法点:**
- 过去进行时的使用
- 简单过去时的叙述

## 4. 梦想的职业

**Title: Dream Jobs**

**Body:**
What do you want to be when you grow up? Lucy wants to be a doctor because she likes helping people. Her brother Tom wants to be a driver. He loves cars. Their cousin Lisa wants to be a nurse. She is kind and caring. Their friend Mike wants to be a police officer. He wants to keep people safe. Everyone has different dreams. It's important to work hard to make them come true.

**中文大意:**
你长大后想成为什么？露西想成为一名医生，因为她喜欢帮助人。她的哥哥汤姆想成为一名司机。他喜欢汽车。他们的表妹丽莎想成为一名护士。她善良又有爱心。他们的朋友迈克想成为一名警察。他想保护人们的安全。每个人都有不同的梦想。努力工作实现梦想很重要。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**

**语法点:**
- 一般现在时的使用
- 条件句的表达

## 5. 丢失的项链

**Title: The Lost Necklace**

**Body:**
One windy day, Emma was playing in the garden. She was wearing her mother's beautiful silver necklace. Suddenly, the wind blew very hard, and the necklace fell off. Emma looked for it everywhere. She checked the bushes and under the flowers. A magpie was watching her. It had the necklace in its beak! Emma asked the magpie to give it back. The magpie flew down and dropped it. Emma was very happy and thanked the magpie.

**中文大意:**
一个有风的日子，艾玛在花园里玩耍。她戴着妈妈的漂亮银项链。突然，风刮得很大，项链掉了。艾玛到处找它。她检查了灌木丛和花下面。一只喜鹊在看着她。它嘴里叼着项链！艾玛让喜鹊把项链还给她。喜鹊飞下来，把项链放下。艾玛非常高兴，感谢了喜鹊。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
windy, garden, beautiful, silver, necklace, look for, bush, flower, magpie

**语法点:**
- 过去进行时与一般过去时的结合
- 简单过去式的使用

## 6. 运动日

**Title: Sports Day**

**Body:**
It was sports day at school. The sun was shining, and the air was cool. The mascot was a friendly panda. There were many races. The athletes ran very fast. The cyclists rode their bikes around the track. Everyone cheered loudly. Jenny won the 100-meter race. She was very excited. Her friends gave her a wristband as a gift. It was a fantastic day for everyone.

学校举行了运动日。阳光明媚，空气凉爽。吉祥物是一只友好的熊猫。有很多比赛。运动员们跑得很快。骑自行车的人绕着跑道骑车。大家都大声欢呼。珍妮赢得了100米赛跑。她非常兴奋。她的朋友们给了她一个腕带作为礼物。对每个人来说，这都是美好的一天。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 简单句的使用

They played games and ate candy. Sarah's mom made a delicious cake. Everyone sang "Happy Birthday" to her. Sarah opened her gifts and thanked everyone. It was a wonderful day filled with friendship and joy.

## 9. 神秘的钥匙

**Title: The Mystery Key**

**Body:**
Tom found a strange key in the garden. He didn't know whose it was. He asked his family, but no one knew. Tom decided to look for the lock that matched the key. He searched around the house and the garden. Finally, he found an old box under the bush. The key fit! Inside, there was a note and a small gift. It was from his grandfather, who had hidden it years ago. Tom was very happy.

**中文大意:**
汤姆在花园里发现了一把奇怪的钥匙。他不知道这是谁的。他问了家人，但没人知道。汤姆决定寻找与钥匙匹配的锁。他在房子和花园周围搜索。最后，他在灌木丛下找到了一个旧盒子。钥匙合适！里面有一张纸条和一个小礼物。是他的祖父几年前藏的。汤姆非常高兴。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
strange, key, whose, look for, lock, bush, grandfather

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 简单句的使用

## 10. 动物园一日游

**Title: A Day at the Zoo**

**Body:**
Last Sunday, the class went to the zoo. They saw many animals - lions, tigers, elephants, and pandas. The guide told them interesting facts about each animal. They watched the penguins swim and the monkeys play. They took photos with the zoo mascot. At lunchtime, they ate sandwiches and fruit. Some students bought souvenirs. It was a fun day. Everyone learned something new about animals.

**中文大意:**
上周日，班级去了动物园。他们看到了许多动物——狮子、老虎、大象和熊猫。导游告诉他们关于每种动物的有趣事实。他们观看企鹅游泳和猴子玩耍。他们与动物园吉祥物合影。午餐时，他们吃了三明治和水果。一些学生买了纪念品。这是有趣的一天。每个人都学到了关于动物的新知识。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
zoo, guide, take photos, mascot, souvenir, learn

**语法点:**
- 一般过去时的叙述
- 简单句的组合

## 11. 雨天的故事

**Title: Rainy Day Story**

**Body:**
It was a rainy day. Lily couldn't go outside to play. She was worried. Then her mom said, "Let's make cookies together." They found the recipe and gathered the ingredients. Lily helped mix the dough. They put the cookies in the oven. While waiting, they read a book about magic. When the cookies were ready, they smelled delicious. Lily's dad came home with an umbrella. They all ate cookies together. It turned out to be a wonderful day.

**中文大意:**
那是一个雨天。莉莉不能出去玩。她很担心。然后她妈妈说：“我们一起做饼干吧。”他们找到了食谱，收集了材料。莉莉帮忙搅拌面团。他们把饼干放进烤箱。等待的时候，他们读了一本关于魔法的书。当饼干做好时，它们闻起来很香。莉莉的爸爸带着雨伞回家了。他们一起吃饼干。这变成了美好的一天。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
umbrella, worried, magic, delicious

**语法点:**
- 过去时态的叙述
- 简单句的使用

## 13. 时间胶囊

**Title: Time Capsule**

**Body:**
The class decided to make a time capsule. They put in letters, photos, and small objects. Each student wrote about their dreams. They included a comic book, a sticker, and a postcard. They buried the capsule in the school garden. They wrote the date on a stone. They agreed to dig it up in 10 years. Everyone was excited to think about what they would be doing then. It was a special project that brought the class together.

**中文大意:**
班级决定制作一个时间胶囊。他们放进了信件、照片和小物件。每个学生都写了关于他们的梦想。他们包括了一本漫画书、一张贴纸和一张明信片。他们把胶囊埋在学校花园里。他们在石头上写下日期。他们约定10年后把它挖出来。每个人都很兴奋地想象那时他们会在做什么。这是一个让班级团结在一起的特殊项目。

**✅ 本篇包含的单词:**
time, comic, sticker, postcard, garden, special

**语法点:**
- 一般过去时与一般将来时的结合
- 简单句的使用
